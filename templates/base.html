Central University - Student Representative Council

The Student Representative Council (SRC) serves as the pulse of the student body, acting as a dedicated platform for representation, advocacy, and community leadership. The SRC plays a vital role in voicing student concerns, driving positive change, and fostering a vibrant campus environment. It operates under the principles outlined in the 2021 SRC Constitution, which delineates the roles and responsibilities of each member, ensuring a cohesive and effective governance structure. It comprises of The Executive Council and the Parliament.

Central University - Parliament

The Parliament is the deliberative body responsible for shaping policies, discussing critical issues, and enacting resolutions that impact our student community. Through transparency, inclusivity, and a commitment to student welfare, the SRC aims to create an environment where every voice is heard, valued, and represented. Join us in this journey of empowerment, engagement, and collaboration as we work together to make our campus a thriving, inclusive, and student-centric community.
