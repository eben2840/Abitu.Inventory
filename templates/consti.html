The Constitution.

The 2021 SRC Constitution stands as the blueprint defining the structure, functions, and responsibilities of our Student Representative Council. It comprehensively outlines the roles and duties of each council member. Moreover, the Constitution details the workings of the Parliament, the highest decision-making body, responsible for deliberating and enacting resolutions that shape our student community.

This document serves as the guiding compass, fostering transparency, accountability, and effective governance within our student body. The Constitution captures the essence of the SRC, emphasizing its significance in guiding the student body, functioning and highlighting its emphasis on structured governance and representation.
