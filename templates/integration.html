Creating an Inventory Account

1. If you are a new user, Click on register on the system.
2. Fill in the required details (such as name, email, password etc..). If you already have an account, simply log in using your credentials.
3. Account Setup: Follow any additional prompts to set up your account preferences, such as email verification.
4. Dashboard: Upon successful login, you will be directed to the login,to login you into your account.

Adding a Categories

1. Navigate to Categories: From the dashboard, locate and click on the "Categories" or "Inventory Categories" section.
2.Add Category: Look for the "Add Category" button and click on it..
3.Enter Category Details: Provide a name and description for the category.
4.Save Category: Click on the "Save" or "Add" button to create and save the category.

Adding Goals and Tasks

1. Navigate to Goals/Tasks: Depending on the system's layout, find and click on either "Goals" or "Tasks" from the dashboard or navigation menu.
2. Add Goal/Task: Look for the "Add Goal" or "Add Task" button and click on it.
3.Set Details: Enter the goal or task details, such as name, description, due date, priority, etc.
4.Assign to Product/Category: Optionally, assign the goal or task to a specific product or category within your inventory.

Adding a Product

1. Navigate to Products: From the dashboard, find and click on the "Products" or "Inventory" tab/menu.
2. Add Product: Look for the "Add Product" button and click on it.
3. Enter Product Details: Fill in the necessary information about the product, such as name, description, quantity, price, SKU (Stock Keeping Unit), etc.
4. Save Product: After entering the details, click on the "Save" or "Add" button to add the product to your inventory.Access the System: Go to the Inventory Management System website or application.

Tracking a Single Product

1. Find Product: Navigate to the "Products" or "Inventory" section to locate the product you want to track.
2. View Product Details: Click on the product name or details to access its dedicated page.
3.Track Inventory: Depending on the system's features, you can track various aspects such as quantity, sales, restocking needs, expiration dates (if applicable), etc.
4.Generate Reports: Utilize the reporting or analytics tools within the system to track and analyze the product's performance over time.
