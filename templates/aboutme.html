CU-SRC Handing Over'24

The Elected Executive Council

It is with immense pride and excitement that we introduce the remarkable
individuals who will steer the ship of our Student Representative Council (SRC) this year.



President: Mr Michael Yeboah, the esteemed voice representing our collective student body in all matters within the jurisdiction of the SRC in advocating for our rights and championing our interests.

Vice President: Miss Ashley Esther Tutu, the President's right-hand individual, supporting the President's duties and taking on assigned tasks with unwavering dedication.

General Secretary: Mr. Dajonang Damyeni, the guardian of official SRC records and preserving the decisions made in SRC meetings and other vital gatherings.

Treasurer: Mr. Lancelott Ben-Akiem , the custodian of our SRC's financial matters, as a signatory to the council's bank accounts, ensuring accountability and transparency in financial operations.

Women's Commissioner: Miss Regina Banfo , a beacon of empowerment for our female student cohort. Harnessing the intellectual potential of our female students and enabling them to be problem-solving agents within our community.

Let us join hands with them, share our ideas, and work collaboratively towards a more vibrant and inclusive campus environment for all.
